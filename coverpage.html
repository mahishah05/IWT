IndiGo Airlines, founded in 2006, is a low-cost carrier based in India. Known for its punctuality, efficient service, and cost-effective operations, IndiGo has rapidly grown to become the largest airline in India by market share.IndiGo focuses on providing a no-frills travel experience, emphasizing affordable fares, on-time performance, and customer convenience. The airline is recognized for its well-maintained fleet, professional staff, and a straightforward service model that appeals to both leisure and business travelers.
